Livable City Initiative

The Livable City Initiative (LCI) is a neighborhood-focused agency with a mission to enhance the experience of the individuals who live and work in the City of New Haven. We achieve this mission and vision by encouraging the involvement of other city agencies, enterprise, and most importantly, the public. Through the cooperative efforts of the public and private sectors, we will create a vibrant ane exciting New Haven for all. Learn more about the agency here.
